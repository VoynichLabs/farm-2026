## Processing Notes

- **Almost every HEIC photo needs 90° rotation** before web use — they were shot in portrait but saved sideways.
- HEIC files must be converted to JPEG or WebP for web use. Use `sips` on the Mac Mini: `sips -s format jpeg -Z 1200 input.HEIC --out output.jpg`
- Keep EXIF data intact (per owner preference).
- Converted preview copies exist at `/tmp/farm_preview/` but these are temporary — reconvert from originals when preparing for production.
