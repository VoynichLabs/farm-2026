# Birdadotta Hatches & TSC Haul — 25-April-2026

## Birdadotta — Birdadonna's Egg Hatches
Big day on the farm. Birdadonna's egg — laid 03-April-2026, set in the incubator the same day — hatched on the morning of 25-April-2026, right around the day-22 mark.

The new chick has been named **Birdadotta**. Brown-and-cream striped down, small orange beak, big dark eye. She zipped clean and is fluffing up in the brooder.

This continues the Birdgit → Birdadette → Birdadonna → Birdadotta name line: each new generation gets a "Bird-" name with one more letter combination layered on.
