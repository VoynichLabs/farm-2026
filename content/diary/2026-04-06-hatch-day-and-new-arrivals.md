## Birdadette Hatches — 06-April-2026

**Incubator conditions at hatch:**
- Temperature: 99.6°F
- Humidity: 69%
- Eggs were marked with set dates (3/16 and 3/17) in purple marker
- Remaining eggs did not hatch
